1 A Practical Primer in Human Complex Genetics

A Practical Primer in Human Complex Genetics was originally written for the the Genetic Epidemiology course organized by the Master Epidemiology of Utrecht University, but can be used as a practical guide and companion to learning and doing a genome-wide association study (GWAS).

It is available as GitBook and HTML. A PDF and EPUB version is forthcoming.

This book was made possible by the kind help of Caspar J. van Lissa who wrote an excellent instruction through an example GitBook https://cjvanlissa.github.io/gitbook-demo/.
